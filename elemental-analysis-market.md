<p><p>The elemental analysis market is segmented into organic and inorganic analysis. Organic elemental analysis focuses on determining the elemental composition of organic compounds, often utilizing combustion techniques to measure carbon, hydrogen, nitrogen, and sulfur content. Inorganic elemental analysis, on the other hand, assesses the elemental composition of inorganic materials, including metals and minerals, using methods like inductively coupled plasma and atomic absorption spectroscopy. Both segments are essential in various industries, including pharmaceuticals, environmental testing, and materials science.</p></p>
