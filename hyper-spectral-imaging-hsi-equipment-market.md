<p><p>The Hyper-Spectral Imaging (HSI) Equipment Market comprises various types based on spectral ranges. Visible/Near Infrared (VNIR) captures images from 400 to 1000 nm, ideal for agriculture and environmental monitoring. Short Wave Infrared (SWIR), covering 1000 to 2500 nm, is effective for mineral identification and moisture detection. Medium Wave Infrared (MWIR) operates from 3000 to 5000 nm, suited for thermal imaging. Long Wave Infrared (LWIR) ranges from 8000 to 14000 nm, primarily for temperature measurement and surveillance. Other markets include specialized applications across various industries.</p></p>
