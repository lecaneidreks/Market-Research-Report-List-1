<p><p>The Portable Emissions Analyzers market is categorized into three main types: Infrared, UV, and Others. Infrared analyzers utilize infrared light to measure gas concentrations and are commonly used for detecting carbon dioxide and hydrocarbons. UV analyzers rely on ultraviolet light to analyze emissions, particularly for measuring ozone and other reactive gases. The "Others" category encompasses alternative technologies, such as electrochemical sensors and laser-based systems, which serve various applications in environmental monitoring and industrial emissions compliance.</p></p>
