<p><p>Electro Dialysis (ED) systems are primarily categorized into two types: Continuous and Batch Electro Dialysis. Continuous Electro Dialysis operates on a constant flow of feed solution, efficiently separating ions and producing concentrated and diluted solutions simultaneously. It is ideal for large-scale applications due to its uninterrupted operation. In contrast, Batch Electro Dialysis processes feed solutions in discrete batches, making it suitable for smaller volumes and varying feed compositions. Both methods leverage electric fields to drive ion migration, facilitating water treatment and resource recovery.</p></p>
