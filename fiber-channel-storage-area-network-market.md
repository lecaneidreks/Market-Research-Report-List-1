<p><p>In the Fiber Channel Storage Area Network (SAN) market, Single-Mode Optical Fiber and Multi-Mode Optical Fiber play crucial roles. Single-Mode Optical Fiber offers higher bandwidth and longer transmission distances, making it ideal for long-range communications with minimal signal loss. In contrast, Multi-Mode Optical Fiber supports shorter distances and is typically more cost-effective, catering to data centers and local area networks. Together, these fiber types facilitate efficient data transfer and storage connectivity, adapting to various application needs in IT infrastructure.</p></p>
