<p><p>The Inferior Vena Cava (IVC) filters market primarily consists of two types: Permanent IVC filters and Retrievable IVC filters. Permanent IVC filters are designed for long-term use, providing ongoing protection against pulmonary embolism in patients with chronic risk factors. In contrast, Retrievable IVC filters offer a temporary solution, allowing for removal once the risk of embolism decreases. This flexibility makes them appealing for patients with short-term thromboembolic risks, catering to varied clinical needs in venous thromboembolism management.</p></p>
