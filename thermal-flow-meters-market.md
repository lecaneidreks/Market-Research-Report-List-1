<p><strong>The Thermal Flow Meters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ultrasonic</li><li>Coriolis</li><li>Turbine</li><li>Vortex</li><li>Magnetic</li><li>Differential Pressure</li><li>Positive Displacement</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermal flow meters market encompasses various technologies for measuring fluid flow. **Ultrasonic flow meters** use sound waves to determine flow rate, while **Coriolis meters** measure mass flow by detecting the change in oscillation frequency. **Turbine meters** utilize rotating blades to gauge flow velocity. **Vortex meters** detect pressure fluctuations caused by vortices. **Magnetic flow meters** measure conductivity in conductive fluids, and **Differential Pressure meters** assess flow based on pressure drop. Lastly, **Positive Displacement meters** calculate volume by trapping fluid in chambers. Each type has unique applications and advantages.</p></p>
