<p><strong>The Buzzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Piezo Buzzers</li><li>Magnetic Buzzer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The buzzer market is primarily segmented into piezo buzzers and magnetic buzzers. Piezo buzzers utilize piezoelectric materials to convert electrical energy into sound, offering advantages like compact size and low power consumption, making them ideal for portable devices. In contrast, magnetic buzzers operate using an electromagnetic system, producing sound through magnetic fields, and are generally favored for their robustness and higher sound output. The choice between these types depends on application requirements, including size, sound quality, and power efficiency.</p></p>
