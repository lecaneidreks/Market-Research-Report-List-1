<p><p>Helical Submerged Arc Welding (HSAW) Steel Pipe is a welding technique where the welding arc is submerged under a layer of granulated flux, allowing for the production of high-quality, durable steel pipes with helical seams. HSAW pipes are primarily utilized in oil and gas transportation, water supply systems, and construction applications due to their superior strength and corrosion resistance.</p><p>The HSAW Steel Pipe Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by the increasing demand for infrastructure development and energy projects globally.
