<p><p>The LED light diffuser films market is primarily divided into two types: multi-sheet and single-sheet. Multi-sheet diffusers consist of several layers that enhance light distribution while minimizing hotspots, making them ideal for applications requiring uniform illumination. Single-sheet diffusers, on the other hand, consist of a single layer and offer a simpler solution for basic lighting needs. Both types serve various industries, including automotive, architecture, and consumer electronics, by improving lighting quality and energy efficiency in LED systems.</p></p>
