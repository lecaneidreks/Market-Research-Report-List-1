<p><ul><li>Pneumatically Driven</li><li>Electrically Powered</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic CPR Devices market is segmented into two main types: pneumatically driven and electrically powered devices. Pneumatically driven devices utilize compressed air to generate chest compressions, offering a consistent and rhythmic force that mimics manual CPR. In contrast, electrically powered devices operate using electric motors to automate the compression and decompression process, ensuring optimal resuscitation efforts. Both types enhance CPR effectiveness by providing hands-free assistance during critical emergencies, improving patient outcomes in cardiac arrest situations.</p></p>
