<p><strong>The Water Descaler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electromagnetic Type</li><li>Capacitive Electronic Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The water descaler market comprises primarily two types: electromagnetic and capacitive electronic. Electromagnetic descalers utilize electromagnetic fields to alter the physical properties of scale-forming minerals, preventing buildup on pipes and appliances. Capacitive electronic descalers, on the other hand, use electrical signals to influence the behavior of minerals in water, reducing their tendency to form unwanted deposits. Both types aim to enhance water quality and extend the lifespan of plumbing systems, catering to varying consumer needs and preferences.</p></p>
