<p><p>The bolted cover swing check valves market features three primary types: flange type, thread type, and socket weld type. Flange type valves are bolted to piping systems, providing strong connections for high-pressure applications. Thread type valves use threaded fittings for easy installation and maintenance, commonly found in low-pressure systems. Socket weld type valves are designed for welded connections, ensuring a tight seal and minimum leakage, making them ideal for high-pressure environments. Each type caters to specific industry needs and pipeline configurations.</p></p>
