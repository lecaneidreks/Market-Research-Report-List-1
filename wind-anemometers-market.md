<p><p>The wind anemometer market is segmented into several types, each serving distinct purposes. Cup anemometers measure wind speed using rotating cups, while vane anemometers utilize a rotating blade to determine both speed and direction. Hot-wire anemometers employ a heated wire to assess wind speed based on cooling effects. Digital anemometers offer electronic readings for accuracy and convenience. Other types may include laser and sonic anemometers, catering to specialized applications in meteorology, aviation, and industrial uses.</p></p>
