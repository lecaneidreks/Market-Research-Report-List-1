<p><p>The plastic ball bearings market comprises several types, including radial ball bearings and deep groove ball bearings. Deep groove ball bearings, characterized by their deep raceway grooves, accommodate both radial and axial loads, offering versatility and higher speed capabilities. Other types of plastic ball bearings may include angular contact bearings and self-aligning ball bearings, catering to diverse industry needs with benefits like corrosion resistance and lightweight properties.</p></p>
