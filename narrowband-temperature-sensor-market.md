<p><p>The narrowband temperature sensor market consists of three main types: resistance sensors, infrared sensors, and other specialized devices. Resistance sensors, such as thermistors and RTDs, determine temperature by measuring changes in resistance. Infrared sensors detect thermal radiation and provide non-contact temperature measurements, ideal for moving or inaccessible objects. Other types may include semiconductor sensors and fiber optic sensors, offering unique advantages in specific applications. Each type caters to diverse industries, including manufacturing, healthcare, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
