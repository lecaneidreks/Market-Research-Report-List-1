SiC MOSFETs are widely used for high-efficiency power management in various applications, offering superior performance at elevated temperatures and voltages. SiC diode devices, particularly Schottky diodes, enable faster switching and reduced conduction losses, making them ideal for power conversion systems. Together, these components enhance energy efficiency and thermal management in industries like renewable energy, electric vehicles, and industrial automation.</p></p>
