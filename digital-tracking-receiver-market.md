<p><p>The digital tracking receiver market is categorized into single-channel and multi-channel types. Single-channel receivers are designed to track one signal at a time, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness for specific applications. In contrast, multi-channel receivers can simultaneously process multiple signals, enhancing data collection and accuracy in complex environments. Multi-channel systems are ideal for applications requiring high reliability and real-time monitoring, while single-channel systems cater to basic tracking needs, making them suitable for less demanding tasks.</p></p>
