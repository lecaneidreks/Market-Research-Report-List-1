<p><ul><li>Pneumatic</li><li>Electric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Fixed Surgical Suction Pump market is primarily segmented into two types: pneumatic and electric. Pneumatic suction pumps utilize air pressure generated by a compressor to create suction, providing reliable performance in various surgical settings. They are often lightweight and easy to transport. Electric suction pumps, on the other hand, rely on electric motors for suction generation. They offer adjustable suction levels and are generally more efficient, making them ideal for procedures requiring precise control. Both types serve crucial roles in ensuring effective fluid removal during surgeries.</p></p>
