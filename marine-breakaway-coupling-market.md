<p><p>The Marine Breakaway Coupling market comprises primarily two types: Petal Valve Marine Breakaway Coupling and Flip-Flap Marine Breakaway Coupling. The Petal Valve type features a design that employs a petal mechanism to quickly close off the flow during a disconnection event, ensuring minimal spillage. In contrast, the Flip-Flap type uses a pivoting flap to achieve a rapid shutoff, effectively preventing leaks. Both designs are crucial for safeguarding marine environments during fuel transfer operations, enhancing safety and compliance with regulations.</p></p>
