<p><strong>The Microwave Infrared Detectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active</li><li>Passive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The microwave infrared detectors market is segmented into active and passive types. Active detectors emit their own energy, typically in the form of microwave signals, to detect infrared radiation from objects, making them suitable for applications like imaging and remote sensing. Passive detectors, on the other hand, rely on incoming radiation from external sources, detecting thermal emissions naturally present in the environment. Both types serve crucial roles in surveillance, environmental monitoring, and industrial applications, catering to diverse technological needs.</p></p>
