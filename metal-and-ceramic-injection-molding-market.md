<p><p>The Metal and Ceramic Injection Molding (MIM and CIM) market encompasses two specialized manufacturing processes. Metal Injection Molding (MIM) combines metal powders with a polymer binder, allowing for the production of complex metal parts with high precision and functionality, often used in automotive and medical applications. Ceramic Injection Molding (CIM), on the other hand, involves similar techniques but utilizes ceramic powders, producing intricate ceramic components prized for their high thermal resistance and electrical insulation properties, commonly found in electronics and aerospace industries.</p></p>
