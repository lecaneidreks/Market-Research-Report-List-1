<p><strong>The Smart Automotive Headlights Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Adaptive Front Lighting Headlight</li><li>Adaptive Driving Beam Headlight</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Smart automotive headlights enhance driving safety and visibility through advanced technologies. The Adaptive Front Lighting System (AFS) adjusts the headlight direction based on vehicle speed and steering angle, optimizing illumination around curves. In contrast, Adaptive Driving Beam (ADB) headlights dynamically control individual LED beams to provide maximum brightness without blinding oncoming drivers by selectively dimming or turning off specific lights. Both systems improve nighttime driving experiences, reduce accidents, and contribute to overall road safety.</p></p>
