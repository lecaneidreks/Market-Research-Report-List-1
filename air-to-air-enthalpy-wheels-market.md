<p><p>The air-to-air enthalpy wheels market is categorized into three types: condensation rotors, hygroscopic rotors, and sorption rotors. Condensation rotors focus on heat and moisture exchange during air transfer, enhancing energy efficiency. Hygroscopic rotors use moisture-absorbing materials to manage humidity levels while recovering energy. Sorption rotors employ materials that can adsorb and desorb moisture, optimizing both temperature and humidity control. Each type plays a crucial role in improving HVAC performance and energy conservation in various applications.</p></p>
