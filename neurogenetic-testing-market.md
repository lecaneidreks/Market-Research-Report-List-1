Next Generation Sequencing (NGS) enables comprehensive genetic analysis, identifying mutations linked to neurological disorders. Multiplex Ligation-dependent Probe Amplification (MLPA) detects copy number variations in multiple genes simultaneously. Fragment analysis PCR focuses on analyzing specific DNA fragments for genetic variations.
