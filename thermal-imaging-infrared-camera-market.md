<p><strong>The Thermal Imaging Infrared Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Short-wave Length Infrared Camera</li><li>Mid-wave Length Camera</li><li>Long-wave Length Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermal imaging infrared camera market consists of three primary types based on wavelength. Short-wave length infrared cameras operate in the 0.9 to 1.7 micrometer range, ideal for high-temperature applications. Mid-wave length cameras, ranging from 3 to 5 micrometers, are commonly used in industrial inspections and surveillance due to their sensitivity to heat. Long-wave length cameras, operating from 8 to 14 micrometers, excel in detecting body heat and are widely used in firefighting and building inspections. Each type serves specific needs across various industries.</p></p>
