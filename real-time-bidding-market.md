<p><ul><li>Open Auction</li><li>Invited Auction</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In real-time bidding (RTB), the open auction market allows any advertiser to bid for ad space, promoting competition and driving prices based on demand. This transparency increases potential reach but may lead to higher costs. Conversely, the invited auction market restricts participation to select advertisers, often leading to more stable pricing. This type fosters relationships and can provide advertisers with better targeting opportunities, but may limit exposure compared to open auctions. Both types serve distinct advertising strategies.</p></p>
