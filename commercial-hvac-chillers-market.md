<p><p>The Commercial HVAC Chillers Market comprises two primary types: air-cooled and water-cooled chillers. Air-cooled chillers utilize ambient air to dissipate heat and are often favored for their ease of installation and lower operational costs. In contrast, water-cooled chillers use water to absorb heat and are typically more efficient, making them suitable for larger commercial applications. Both types serve to regulate building temperatures, ensuring comfort while optimizing energy use in various commercial settings.</p></p>
