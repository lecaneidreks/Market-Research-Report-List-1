<p><p>The Display Test Equipment market is primarily categorized into two types: Array Process Testing Equipment and Cell/Module Process Testing Equipment. Array Process Testing Equipment focuses on evaluating the performance and quality of display arrays during production, ensuring pixel integrity and alignment. In contrast, Cell/Module Process Testing Equipment is designed for testing individual cells or completed modules, assessing factors like brightness, color accuracy, and overall functionality. Together, these equipment types play a crucial role in maintaining display quality in consumer electronics.</p></p>
