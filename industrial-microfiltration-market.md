<p><ul><li>Air Microfiltration</li><li>Liquid Microfiltration</li><li>Oil Microfiltration</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial microfiltration market is categorized into three main types: air microfiltration, liquid microfiltration, and oil microfiltration. Air microfiltration targets the removal of particulates and pollutants from the air, enhancing air quality in various industries. Liquid microfiltration focuses on separating suspended solids and microorganisms from liquids, used widely in water treatment and food processing. Oil microfiltration efficiently separates impurities from oils and lubricants, prolonging equipment life and maintaining performance across industries. Each type plays a vital role in improving operational efficiency and environmental safety.</p></p>
