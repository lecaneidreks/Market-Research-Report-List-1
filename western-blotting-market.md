<p><p>Western Blotting is a widely used analytical technique in molecular biology and biochemistry for the detection and quantification of specific proteins in a sample. By utilizing gel electrophoresis to separate proteins based on size, the technique then transfers these proteins onto a membrane where they are probed with antibodies specific to the target proteins. This method aids in various applications, including disease diagnosis, research studies, and protein expression analyses.</p><p>The Western Blotting Market is anticipated to experience significant growth, driven by the increasing prevalence of diseases such as cancer and infectious diseases which necessitate advanced diagnostic tools. Ongoing advancements in technology, including the development of automated systems and enhancements in antibody specificity, are further propelling market expansion.
