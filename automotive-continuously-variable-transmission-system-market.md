<p><ul><li>Belt-Chain Drive CVT</li><li>Planetary CVT</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT) System market features two primary types: Belt-Chain Drive CVT and Planetary CVT. Belt-Chain Drive CVTs utilize a system of belts and pulleys to provide seamless gear ratio changes, enhancing fuel efficiency and smooth acceleration. In contrast, Planetary CVTs employ a set of gears arranged in a planetary configuration, allowing for multiple gear ratios without discrete shifting. Both types aim to improve vehicle performance and fuel economy, catering to the evolving demands of the automotive industry.</p></p>
