<p><ul><li>Top-Running</li><li>Bottom-Running</li><li>Side-Running</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Current collectors for Light Rail Trains (LRT) are critical for power transmission. In the top-running market, collectors are mounted above the track, utilizing pantographs to draw overhead electricity, ideal for high-speed systems. Bottom-running setups feature collectors positioned beneath the vehicle, utilizing the track’s conductive elements, often seen in systems where overhead structures are impractical. Side-running collectors are mounted on the side of the train, allowing for flexible installation options. Each type caters to specific infrastructure and operational needs.</p></p>
