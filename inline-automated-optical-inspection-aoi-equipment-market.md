<p><p>Inline Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) equipment is vital in the manufacturing sector, focusing on quality control for printed circuit boards (PCBs). The market is primarily divided into two types: 2D AOI equipment and 3D AOI equipment. </p><p>2D AOI equipment captures images from various angles to detect defects using grayscale and color analysis, making it suitable for simple inspections. In contrast, 3D AOI equipment employs sophisticated techniques like laser triangulation to evaluate solder joint heights and component placement, providing a more comprehensive quality assessment.</p></p>
