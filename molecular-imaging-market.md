<p><ul><li>PET</li><li>PET-CT</li><li>PET-MR</li><li>SPECT</li><li>MR Spectroscopy</li><li>Molecular Ultrasound Imagin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Molecular imaging encompasses various technologies used for visualizing biological processes at the molecular level. PET (Positron Emission Tomography) captures metabolic activity using radioactive tracers. PET-CT combines PET with computed tomography for detailed anatomical mapping. PET-MR merges PET with magnetic resonance imaging, offering functional and structural insights. SPECT (Single Photon Emission Computed Tomography) provides 3D imaging of radiotracer distribution. MR spectroscopy analyzes metabolic changes in tissues. Molecular ultrasound imaging utilizes ultrasound techniques to visualize cellular processes, enhancing diagnosis and treatment monitoring across conditions.</p></p>
