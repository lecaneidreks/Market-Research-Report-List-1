<p><p>The ICU transport ventilator market consists of three main types: electronic, pneumatic, and electro-pneumatic. Electronic ventilators use advanced technology for precise control and monitoring, ensuring optimal patient care in transport settings. Pneumatic ventilators operate using compressed air, making them lightweight and reliable, but with less flexibility in settings. Electro-pneumatic models combine both technologies, offering versatility and efficiency by using electronic controls with pneumatic drive systems, catering to diverse clinical needs during patient transport.</p></p>
