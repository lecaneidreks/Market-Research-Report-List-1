<p><strong>The Threaded Pipe Fittings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Internal Thread</li><li>External Thread</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The threaded pipe fittings market is categorized into internal thread and external thread fittings. Internal thread fittings have threads on the inside, facilitating connections with external-threaded pipes or valves, ideal for creating secure seals and connections in plumbing and industrial applications. Conversely, external thread fittings feature threads on the outside, allowing them to be screwed into internal-threaded fittings, enhancing versatility in assembly. Both types are crucial for ensuring leak-proof connections in various hydraulic, pneumatic, and plumbing systems.</p></p>
