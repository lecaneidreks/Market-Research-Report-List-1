<p><p>The automotive auto-dimming mirror market comprises two main types: outer auto-dimming mirrors and inside auto-dimming mirrors. Outer auto-dimming mirrors, usually found on vehicles' side mirrors, adjust their tint in response to glare from headlights of trailing vehicles, enhancing nighttime visibility and safety. Inside auto-dimming mirrors, typically installed on windshields, reduce glare from rear headlights, promoting driver comfort. Both types utilize advanced electrochromatic technology to improve overall driving experiences, particularly during low-light conditions.</p></p>
