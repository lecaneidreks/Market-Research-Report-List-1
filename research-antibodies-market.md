<p><p>The research antibodies market primarily consists of monoclonal and polyclonal antibodies. Monoclonal antibodies are engineered to recognize a specific target, offering high specificity and consistency, making them ideal for diagnostics and therapeutics. In contrast, polyclonal antibodies are derived from multiple immune cell clones, providing a broader range of target recognition but with variability in quality and specificity. Both types play crucial roles in research applications, including disease detection, drug development, and cell signaling studies.</p></p>
