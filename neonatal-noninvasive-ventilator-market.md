<p><p>The neonatal noninvasive ventilator market includes two primary types: intensive care ventilators and portable/transportable ventilators. Intensive care ventilators are designed for use in hospital settings, providing advanced respiratory support to critically ill neonates. They offer features like high-frequency oscillatory ventilation and precise monitoring capabilities. In contrast, portable/transportable ventilators are designed for mobility and can be used in various settings, including transport to other facilities or in emergency situations, ensuring that neonatal patients receive consistent care on the go.</p></p>
